Privacy Policy — onetab

Effective date:

What onetab does

onetab is a Chrome extension that helps you manage Tasks, a Gratitude journal, a Focus (Pomodoro) timer, and the 369 manifestation method — all locally in your browser.

Data collection & storage

  • No external servers: The extension does not send your data to our servers. It uses Chrome storage (chrome.storage.local) to keep your entries on your device.
  • No sale/sharing: We do not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.
  • Permissions: Only the minimal permissions required for core features are requested.

Remote code

onetab does not load or execute remote code. All code runs from the packaged extension you install from the Chrome Web Store.

Single purpose

The extension’s sole purpose is to provide productivity features (tasks, journaling, focus timer, and 369 method) within your new tab experience.

User controls

  • You can view, edit, and clear your data at any time in the extension UI.
  • Uninstalling the extension removes its stored data from your browser.
